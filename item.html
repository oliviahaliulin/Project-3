SOLERA CARAMEL SHERRY CAKE

Signature Dessert of Solera House

A silky, olive-oil sponge soaked in a warm Pedro Ximénez sherry syrup inspired by the traditional solera aging process. The cake is layered with a light vanilla-bean crema, topped with a glossy sherry-caramel glaze, and finished with toasted almonds for texture. Each slice carries deep, sun-kissed flavors that echo the slow blending and maturation of solera-aged wines.
