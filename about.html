ABOUT SOLERA HOUSE

Solera House was created as a place where Mediterranean warmth meets modern, easygoing hospitality. Inspired by the rhythms of Spanish tapas culture, we focus on simple ingredients prepared with care and meant to be shared. Our kitchen leans into vibrant Mediterranean flavors, while our wine program celebrates the regions that shaped them—Spain, Portugal, and the rich vineyards of South America. The space is designed to feel welcoming from the moment you walk in: soft light, natural textures, and an atmosphere that encourages long conversations. Solera House isn’t just a place to eat—it’s a space to gather, unwind, and enjoy food the way it’s meant to be enjoyed: together.

MEANING BEHIND THE NAME “SOLERA HOUSE”

The word “Solera” refers to a traditional aging method used in Spain for wines and sherries, where new and old vintages blend over time to create depth and complexity. We chose the name to represent the same idea: a place where tradition and modern flavor come together, and where every visit adds to the experience. “House” reflects our goal of creating a welcoming, comfortable space—one that feels familiar, warm, and lived-in the moment you walk through the door.
