SOLERA HOUSE

Solera House is a Mediterranean-inspired wine and tapas bar built around bold flavors and shared dining. We offer rustic small plates, seasonal dishes, and classic tapas meant to be enjoyed together. Our curated wine list highlights Spanish, Portuguese, and South American producers with expressive regional styles. The atmosphere is warm, intimate, and designed for relaxed, social evenings. At Solera House, good food, good wine, and good company come together naturally.

“Solera House instantly became my new favorite spot. Every dish felt fresh, thoughtful, and perfect for sharing, and the wine recommendations were on point. The atmosphere is warm and intimate without feeling crowded, and the staff makes you feel like you’ve been coming here for years. My friends and I stayed for hours just talking, snacking, and trying new wines. Easily one of the best dining experiences I've had in a long time.”

— Elena M.
